About the organizers

What IT Association does!

IT Association under the Department Information Technology, SRM Institute of Science and Technology is one of the oldest and most active associations of the Institute which not only works towards the developments of the students but also looks into the progress of the society at large. Our expertise lies in not only conducting technical events but also workshops, tech talks and non technical events thus providing a great exposure to the students and the participants. Our sister organization, Initiating Fusion, is a student initiated Group which aims at bringing a change and also impacting the society. It is a nonprofit venture which is run by the students. On the technical front, we take pride in having initiated the legacy of Hackathons in SRM in the year 2016 which being the first edition of SRM Hackathon and are looking forward to hosting many more successful Hackathons.
